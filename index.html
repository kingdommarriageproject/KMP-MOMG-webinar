About The Ministry

A ministry built for
this generation's marriages

Marriage today is not the same as it was for our parents — and pretending it is may be one of the reasons so many unions are failing. The Kingdom Marriage Project was built to address this gap. Through monthly live webinars, we provide biblical clarity, practical wisdom, and honest dialogue for singles, couples, and married individuals who want more than feelings — they want a covenant that lasts.

📖

Rooted in Scripture

Every session draws from the Word of God — applying timeless biblical principles to the pressures and complexities of modern marriage.

🏛️

Covenant, Not Contract

We restore marriage to its true identity: a sacred, purpose-driven covenant before God — not a feeling-based social arrangement.

💬

Honest Dialogue

No judgment, no pretending. A safe space for questions people are afraid to ask publicly — and the answers they genuinely need.

🔄

Monthly Sessions

Every last Saturday of the month — a new topic, a fresh conversation, always grounded in the same unchanging kingdom truth.

Who This Is For
  • 🌱 Young adults (18+) exploring life and love
  • 💭 Singles considering relationships or marriage
  • 💑 Individuals currently in relationships
  • 👫 Married couples strengthening their union
  • 🔍 Anyone seeking truth and wisdom about marriage
  • 🙏 Those who want guidance, not just opinions
